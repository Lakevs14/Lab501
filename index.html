Diving Into Calories

Nutrition is an often neglected aspect of exercise science that many individuals do not recognize the importance of. The fact is that calories, along with the distribution of our macronutrients, will determine our muscle density, weight, and performance outcome.



The following table on the right hand side will demonstrate real life scenarios. You will notice that a female at an average height exercising more than a male at an average height will burn less calories.


Gender Age Activity Height Weight Maintenace Calories
Male 28 Light Activity 5'10 167 2500
Female 28 Very Active 5'4 115 2273
